---
title: 'Bread Pudding'
date: '04:11 16-08-2021'
---

Ingredients.

Loaf  of bread preferably a couple of days old.
2 medium eggs
4 oz sugar
6ish ozs sultanas
                Currants
                Raisins

Mixed spice to suit
Tblspoon or so of oil to grease roasting tin



Loaf of  bread with the crusts removed, soaked in water for a few minutes in a colander in a bowl.

Remove the colander from the bowl,squeeze the water gently out of the bread.

Add all the  other ingredients and mix well.

Add to the heated roasting tin and place in the oven for one and a half hours approximately at 200c.   Check the top  is quite firm but not burnt.

Good luck!

# Bread Pudding

    <img alt="Bread Pudding" src="../../assets/images/breadpudding.jpg" title="Bread Pudding">
    <figcaption class="polaroid-caption">Eat warm or cold</figcaption>
</figure>
!!! info "Ingredients"

    - Loaf  of bread preferably a couple of days old.
    - 2 medium eggs
    - 4 oz sugar
    - 6ish ozs sultanas
    - Currants
    - Raisins
    - Mixed spice to suit
    - Tblspoon or so of oil to grease roasting tin

!!! success "METHOD"

    - Take a loaf of  bread with the crusts removed, soaked in water for a few minutes in a colander in a bowl.

    - Remove the colander from the bowl,squeeze the water gently out of the bread.

    - Add all the  other ingredients and mix well.

    - Add to the heated roasting tin and place in the oven for one and a half hours approximately at 200c.   
    - Check the top  is quite firm but not burnt.

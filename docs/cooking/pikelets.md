I originally had the recipe off:-

BakingMad.com

450gm Strong white flour
7gm quick bake yeast
300ml water
300ml Plenish Almond Drink (I am Lactose intolerant)
1tsp Sugar
1tsp Salt

1 Warm Almond Drink and Water together

2 Place all the ingredients into a bowl and beat until smooth.  Put to
one side until frothy and the size has doubled.

3 Grease a heavy frying pan and some 4 inch egg rings. Heat.

4 Half fill each ring.

5 Cook the crumpets until the surface bubbles (about 5 minutes). Reduce
heat until the bubbles have burst.

6 Turn the crumpets over and for further time until the edges of the
bubbles start to brown, slightly.

7 Re-grease, increase the heat and repeat.

There you are, easier than pancakes.

Having been on shift work a while and needing some pasta added to the meal list we bought one of these :![611wetoMM8L._AC_SL1500_](images/611wetoMM8L._AC_SL1500_.jpg "611wetoMM8L._AC_SL1500_") Lékué Recipiente Quick Pasta

125g of Pasta and 300ml of water cooked for 10 minutes gives perfect al-dente pasta for one, just add meat and sauce, or in my case after the dentist visit just add grated cheese. 

More amounts later as during the winter we will work on more varieties and cooking times.
